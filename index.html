BASSTATIC x JOE NICE

17 July 2026 · Ash, Nuanu, Bali
6 PM till late

Joe Nice returns from the US to Basstatic. After an unforgettable debut and the energy you gave him, coming back was the only option. Heavyweight bass. Pure soundsystem culture. See you in front of the stacks.

🎟️ Early Bird tickets available this week only.

Joe Nice

The Return · Dubstep · 140

North America's first dubstep DJ returns. Playing the sound since 2001, before it even had a name — co-founder of Dub War NYC, the first dubstep night in North America, and the first American DJ on Rinse FM, at DMZ, and at FWD>>.

Over two decades of global touring and an unshakeable commitment to the deep, dark, and heavy end of the spectrum. After his unforgettable Basstatic debut, the only move was to bring him back.

Joining him is Hoda Fadel, making her long-awaited debut bass music set — a special milestone we're proud to share with you. Completing the journey are the Basstatic resident selectors: Muvs, Roza Mozes, Roba Grow and Vokvi. Hosted by MC Nyoman.

What is Basstatic

Basstatic is a cultural movement centered around bass, movement, and inner state. A space where music becomes a tool for release, expression, and transformation. Through deep bass and conscious movement, the dance becomes a form of meditation, a reset, and a way to return to an authentic state.
