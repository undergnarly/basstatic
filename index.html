BASSTATIC x MUNGK

22 May 2026 · Ash, Nuanu, Bali
6 PM till late

UK bass meets world folk. A night of deep dubstep, 140 and dub with Mungk (UK), Roba Grow, Muvs, Roza Mozes, Vokvi, Dame Bambou and MC Nyoman. No resistance. Just bass.

Mungk

UK Bass · World Folk · DUPLOC

British-born DJ and producer Matt Beauchamp has spent nearly a decade crafting a singular blend of UK bass music and world folk sounds. Now touring Asia full time, Mungk turns the soundsystem into a vehicle for travel — bass-heavy, dubwise, and deeply melodic.

His debut album Temple of Mungk on DUPLOC follows a monk's journey from China through India, the Middle East, Egypt and Africa — woven with field recordings and traditional instrumentation from each country. A concept project that turned heads across the deep dubstep scene worldwide.

Supported by Commodo, Sicaria Sound, Truth, J:Kenzo, Hatcha, N-Type, Ternion Sound and Joe Nice — Mungk arrives in Bali for an intimate Basstatic showcase. Come early. Stay deep.

What is Basstatic

Basstatic is a cultural movement centered around bass, movement, and inner state. A space where music becomes a tool for release, expression, and transformation. Through deep bass and conscious movement, the dance becomes a form of meditation, a reset, and a way to return to an authentic state.
